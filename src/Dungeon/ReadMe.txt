Welcome to the Dungeon, player.

Start the game by running the Launcher file.
Then, enter your name.

Use L and R (and U and D when appropriate) to move within the dungeon.
Use PRINT to print your current floor. 
Your position is represented by "Y."
The enemies are represented by "X."
The entrance and exit of the current floor are represented respectively by "I" and "O."
When you are at an entrance or exit, use EXIT to respectively move to the previous or next floor. 
Every 6th floor is a shop floor, where you can use an INTEGER like "100" to buy that amount of a certain stat. 
Use STATS to print your current stats.

The currency in this world is called "evil."
